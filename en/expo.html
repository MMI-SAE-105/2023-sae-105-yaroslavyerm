Exposition

Discover the exhibition "Kahn: Form and Function" - a tribute to the architectural brilliance of Louis Kahn. Immerse yourself in visionary designs, from the famous Salk Institute to the timeless National Assembly Building in Bangladesh. Join us on a journey through the narrative of an architect whose work continues to shape the architectural landscape. Follow us to celebrate the enduring power of visionary design.
